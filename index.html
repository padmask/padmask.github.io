I am a Hale Visiting Assistant Professor at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am interested in algebraic geometry and number theory. I received my PhD in 2016 at MIT under the supervision of Bjorn Poonen. My undergraduate and masters degrees are from Chennai Mathematical Institute.
